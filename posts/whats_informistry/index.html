What's Informistry?

Posted by Hank Lee on Saturday, May 2, 2020

When I tell others that my major subject in college is chemistry and biochemistry, both of them are shocked.

They reckon the knowledge of chemistry and that of IT are totally different, and I always agree with that.

For me, chemistry is fun, but maybe information technology is the career which I intend to devote my whole life to.

So, the word Informistry is a combination of my life:

  • Now: the career I work on now: Information Technology
  • Past: the major subject in college: Chemistry
    Informistry = IT + Chemistry

This website is used to record those knowledge and tips that I cannot remember🤣.
